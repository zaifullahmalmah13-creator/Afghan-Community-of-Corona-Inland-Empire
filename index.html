About Us

Afghan Community Organization is a small nonprofit focused on practical, community-based support for Afghan families and newcomers. We work with local partners to connect families to services, information, and opportunities.

Our approach is simple: listen first, protect privacy, and provide clear steps to help people stabilize and move forward.

Mission

To strengthen Afghan families through trusted support, education, and pathways to stability.

Values

  • Respect and confidentiality
  • Community-led support
  • Transparency and accountability
